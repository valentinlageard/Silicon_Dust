Psychosurgery

Psychosurgery is the art of performing operations directly into the ego.

When you get this expertise, you get the Gear and assets, the Gigs and a Psychosurgery move of your choice.

Gear and assets

By default, you get :

  • A +1-aug computation chip : +1 Process.
  • An ego loader, external or integrated (1-aug).

Additionally, choose 1 :

  • Another +1-aug computation chip : +1 Process.
  • A daemon with dynamic ego falsification allowing you to bypass common identification trackers. Contrary to most people, what you do is not publicly available information. It still may be retrieved by motivated and competent individuals.
  • A custom weapon worth 2-cred.

Gigs

As a psychosurgeon you have access to gigs such as simulspace torture of an individual, accelerated psychotherapy for rich people, implanting commands in targets, merging forks, ...

Psychosurgery moves

Accelerated psychotherapy (Psychosurgery) [+Process]

When you perform psychosurgery to heal the mind, roll +Process. If at least a week passed between sessions and you were able to perform accelerated psychotherapy in the meantime on someone, you can roll this move at the beginning of the session.

Success : You heal 2-trauma.

Partial : You heal 1-trauma.

Miss : You inflict 1-trauma.

Behavioral rewiring (Psychosurgery) [+Process]

When you perform psychosurgery to implant a command or modify a behavior, roll +Process.

Success : You implant a command or suppress a behavioural tendency.

Partial : Same as a Success but if the target is unwilling, they suffer 1-trauma.

Miss : The rewiring is unsuccessful and you inflict 1-trauma.

For PCs : When they go against their rewiring, they must Act under pressure to mitigate the effects.

Merging (Psychosurgery) [+Process]

When you perform psychosurgery to merge two egos, roll +Process.

Success : The merging works. The merged ego gets 2 experience.

Partial : The merging mostly works but the cognitive dissonance inflicts 1-trauma.

Miss : The merging fails. It inflicts 2-trauma.

For PCs : Merge failure may mean multiple personality syndrom. They may have to Act under pressure to keep control at critical moments.

Mnemonics (Psychosurgery) [+Process]

When you perform psychosurgery to retrieve or alter memories, roll +Process.

Success : You retrieve semantic or episodic memories, you may alter or delete them or may add artificial memories. The patient will be mostly unsuspecting except if faced with contradictions.

Partial : Same as a success but the patient will have confusing flashes and may retrieve its memory at some point.

Miss : Same as a partial but the patient suffers 1-trauma and will retrieve its memory soon.

For PCs : When they are faced with a contradiction or something susceptible to make them remember, they may Act under pressure to mitigate the mnemonics operation.

Simulspace torture (Psychosurgery)

When you torture an ego in a simulspace, for each question you ask, if they resist (MC's choice for NPC and PC's choice for their character), you can inflict them 1-trauma and they have to tell you. If they suffer too much trauma, well, you definitely broke their mind.

Experimental psychosurgery (Psychosurgery)

Say what you want to accomplish. The MC must say yes but chooses as many as they want : - It'll take hours/days/weeks/months. - You'll need ... first. -
