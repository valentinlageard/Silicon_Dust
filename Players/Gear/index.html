Gear and assets

Creds

In transhuman time, there are multiple currencies all embedded in an intricate blockchain market ecosystem. What counts as money varies a lot in the different habitats : some habitats use labor time as the measure of money, in others trust and community service is money, in others fame and influence is money, ... But all these things end up at some point digitalized in a block of a local blockchain and all blockchains permeates others through more or less legal cryptomarkets. In mechanical terms, currency is measured as cred. Everything that has a price costs some cred. Creds can be acquired as services for the community in autonomous societies, as work for a client or a corporation, as information or entertainment, ... Creds are stored in virtual encrypted wallets accessible only if you got the private keys.

Cred is represented with the tag n-cred.

Gear tags

Gear have tags which represents facts about the gear, limitations, special rules and capacities, ... Here is a list of all gear tags :

  • area : This item ignores gang armor.
  • n-ap : The harm inflicted by this item ignores n armor. If the tag is only ap, it ignores all armor.
  • n-armor : When you suffer harm, you substract armor to the harm received.
  • automatic : You can spend 1-ammo to inflict +1-harm or to have the area tag.
  • braced : This technology must be attached to a brace point to be used.
  • breach : The harm it deals cuts holes in hard material (walls, vehicles, ...).
  • broken : This gear is broken. It cannot be used until it's repaired.
  • clumsy : A big and heavy item. It can't be stealthy and it's almost certainly loud.
  • dangerous : On a miss, the user suffers harm.
  • destroyed : This item is broken and cannot be repaired.
  • emp : This device creates a strong electromagnetic pulse that fries electronics. Against a synthshell, it does double damage. Against a bioshell, it breaks at least one aug (+broken). Against an unprotected device, it fries it.
  • faraday : It's protected against the effect of emp.
  • hand, close, near, far, away : The range of the gear. Hand means melee, close means a dozen meters, near means a few dozens meters, far means a hundred meters, away means a few hundreds meters.
  • n-harm : Inflicts n harm (see harm).
  • loud : Attracts attention by making big noise, big light, big variation in electromagnetic fields, ...
  • mutilate : When someone suffers harm from this item, they automatically loose a limb.
  • off-grid : This item is not wirelessly connected to the Grid. You must have a physical attack vector against it.
  • overload : This gear overloads the target's sensors, they are malfunctionning until calibrated again. As a PC, you have -1-ongoing and can't roll +Sensors moves until you have time to calibrate again. For NPCs, this technology blinds them except if they have countermeasures.
  • repair : This gear is easily repaired : it costs no cred and takes little time.
  • retrieve : This gear only has 1-ammo but you can retrieve it. If you're still here after the fight, you get your ammo back. If you want to retrieve your ammo during a fight, you must Act under pressure.
  • seeker : This object projectiles automatically correct their trajectories to hit the designated target. You have +1 when you Deploy violence using this item.
  • stealthy : Replaces loud. It means that this gear is hidden and only a thorough search will reveal it.
  • n-stun : Stun harm that incapacitates non-lethally, it replaces harm.
  • n-use : This item has a number of uses.
  • vector : When this item is in contact with an off-grid target, it counts as an attack vector for Hacking purposes. This tag is mostly for ammo : shoot a bullet into an off-grid target and you may hack them.

Shells

Shells are the transhuman bodies. Since the invention of cortical stacks, the concept of individuality shifted to differentiate even more the difference between bodies (shells) and minds (ego). There are 3 main types of shells :

  • Infoshells are virtual "bodies" connected to the Grid. Infoshells consist of a Mind Processing Unit that is usually connected to the Grid. As infoshells are not really a physical body, they cannot have usual augmentations. A infoshell character may not do physical actions and moves but still may have effects on the real world through the Grid. However, an infoshell may have a +Process or +Memetics bonuses as augs as if it was a Computation chip or a Social utils and has an equivalent of Grid inserts if it has a connection to the Grid. All physical shells (bio and synth) emulate infoshells when they connect to the Grid through Grid inserts. In simulspaces, infoshells can look like almost anything in the constraints of the simulspace programs.
  • Bioshells are biological bodies. Bioshells may come through organic reproduction of compatible bioshells or may be clones fast grown in vats. Almost all bioshells are genetically fixed from congenital disorders and have basic biological augmentations such as a reinforced immunitary system. Bioshells
  • Synthshells are synthetic bodies. They are made of metals and artificial composites.

A "naked" synthshell costs 2-cred while a "naked" bioshell costs 3-cred. Add to that the corresponding cred of the basic augs it has and you have the final price. Directly buying shells isn't cheap and that's the reason for resurrection insurances.

Augs

Augs are devices directly integrated into a shell.

A n-aug cost depends on the following rules :

  • A 0-aug costs nothing.
  • A 1-aug costs 2-cred.
  • A 2-aug costs 5-cred upfront. If you already have an equivalent 1-aug, it costs 3-cred to upgrade.
  • A 3-aug costs 10-cred upfront. If you already have an equivalent 2-aug, it costs 5-cred to upgrade.

Basic augs

  • Aesthetics augs 0-aug : Aesthetics augmentations. Choose any : facial reconstruction, fur, biosimulated appearance, dynamic tatoos, programmable skin pigmentation, cat ears, frontal horns, programmable hairs, ...
  • Computation chip n-aug : Adds +n Process (max +3). Choose n : quantum processor, cloud computing, bruteforce units, heuristics encoding, math and logic solvers, ...
  • Defence measures n-aug : Adds +n-armor (max +3). Choose n : epidermic kevlar, nanocarbon-reinforced bones, magnetic decelerator, ...
  • Enhanced strength n-aug : Adds +n-harm (max +3) when inflicting harm with your strength. Choose n : synthetic muscles, high newton actuators, ...
  • Fast healing / Autorepair 1-aug : Your body heals or repair itself fast. Whenever an hour or two passes, you erase all tracks on your Harm Clock.
  • Grid inserts 0-aug : Grid inserts is the basic package of almost every shells. Grid inserts comprises a wireless connection to the Grid and the integration of the Grid to the shell's sensory systems. As such, it allows you to access the Grid directly or through your angel, to jack in the Grid and simulspaces, to make Grid related moves, to perceive the layers of alternate reality covering real places, ...
  • Integrated weapon 1-aug : Design a weapon according to the rules : it costs +1-cred to be integrated.
  • Kinesthesic enhancements n-aug : Adds +n Kinesics (max +3). Choose n : nervous autostimulation, internal optic fiber wiring, Thz streaming, fast-access actuators, adrenaline regulator, hyperthreading, ...
  • Motricity actuators 1-aug : All shells ships with a free basic motricity actuator which are generally legs. For different or additional modes of transportation, choose 1 : flight (wings, propeller, ...), swim (fins, propeller, ...), wall climbing (grappling, magnetic crampon, adhesive limbs, ...), ...
  • Sensors array n-aug : Adds +n to Sensors (max +3). Choose n : magnifying vision, IR and UV vision, echolocation, electromagnetic sensitivity, radiation sensor, organic composites sorter, ...
  • Social utils n-aug : Adds +n to Memetics (max +3). Choose n : facial recognition, body-language analyser, pheromone synthesizer, memetic lexer, predictive discourse, personality correlator, ...

Specific augs

  • Antimatter bomb 2-aug 6-harm ap breach area far : When you want or just before dying, you can activate an internal antimatter bomb that deals 6-harm ap breach area in a far range. It kills you instantaneously, destroys your cortical stack as well as the cortical stacks of every shells killed by the explosion. Integrated antimatter bombs are rare and usually forbidden and almost exclusively used suicide missions and terrorism.
  • Ballistics softwares 1-aug : Aiming correction and predictive ballistic stabilization giving a bonus of +1 when you're Deploying violence with a ranged weapon.
  • Brace point 1-aug : You have a retractable gyroscopic arm that allows you to use braced directly on you shell.
  • Close combat softwares 1-aug : Softwares such as kinesthesic analyses or adversary positioning prediction giving a bonus of +1 when you're Deploying violence with melee weapons or with "bare hands".
  • Dead switch 1-aug : A small box containing a small nanoswarm designed to destroy the cortical stack on command or when the shell dies. This aug is used when you don't want your ego to fall in wrong hands.
  • Extreme adaptation mods n-aug : Allows you to ignore certain kind of dangers and damages. For each n, your shell is adapted to 2 : extreme heat, extreme cold, 0g or low-g, high-g, void and high-pressure, radiation, under water, ... Synth shells are automatically adapted to low and high pressures.
  • Faraday net 1-aug +faraday : A faraday net covers your shell protecting you against the effect of emp weapons.
  • Fractal digits 1-aug : A set of fractally dividing digits to manipulate matter at the molecular level.
  • Integrated ego loader* 1-aug : Allows you to load an cortical stack directly into your integrated simulspace. This aug allows you to interact in this simulspace with the ego and perform Psychosurgery*** on them. You can control whether the ego has access or not to your perceptions and you can give them control of your shell.
  • Integrated electron impulser 1-aug : Allows to manipulate off-grid technology at a close range.
  • Quantum relay 2-aug : A quantum relay allows you to instantaneously upload your ego, Angel and software to a server anywhere in the universe when you die. This allows to always retain the memory of your last moments.
  • Stealth enhancements* 1-aug : This augmentations usually comes as sheets of epidermic metamaterials and silenced actuators giving a bonus of +1 to Infiltrate***.
  • Throwing mods n-aug : You can throw anything at superhuman ranges and precision (1-aug : close/near, 2-aug : close/near/far, 3-aug : close/near/far/away).

Resurrection insurance

Since ego technology has appeared, resurrection insurance were put in place. The principle is simple, you regularly upload your ego in a server used as a backup and when you die, the insurance loads your backup in a new shell if it hasn't retrieved your cortical stack. Various levels of insurance allow for various levels of service.

In mechanical terms you must pay your insurance at the beginning of the session. You can select any level of insurance as long as you have the cred to pay it. If you die during the session, you benefit from the insurance, if you don't die, well, too bad.

Resurrection insurance levels :

  • 0-cred resurrection insurance : Regular backup and minimal infoshell processing in case you die.
  • 1-cred resurrection insurance : Regular backup and 2-aug shell, the MC describes its type and look.
  • 2-cred resurrection insurance : Regular backup and a4-aug shell, you detail its type and look.
  • 3-cred resurrection insurance : Regular backup and an identical shell with as many aug as you had when died or a tailored 6-aug shell, you detail its type and look.

Angel/Daemon

An angel is an intelligent device that allows you to access and retrieve information, connect to other devices, communicate with the Grid, run your softwares, ... It's essentially a intelligent multi-purpose computer. It can be a portable device with a screen and buttons or can be an integrated aug that interfaces with your augmented reality. Often, it has an intelligent interface that is a pseudofork of its owner.

A daemon is exactly the same thing except it's designed to anonymize its user and encrypt its communications. Some models even control your angel to make it look like you're elsewhere doing something else. Common in societies that value privacy but forbidden in authoritarian regimes.

Weapons

Firearms

When you create a firearm, choose a frame, its projectile(s) and its mod(s), name it and pay for it. It's always 1-cred + additional creds for additional projectiles and mods.

Frame (choose 1) :

  • Handgun : 2-harm close/near 2-ammo loud
  • Shotgun : 3-harm area close 2-ammo loud
  • Assault rifle : 3-harm close/near automatic 2-ammo loud
  • Machine gun : 4-harm close/near automatic braced clumsy 2-ammo loud
  • Long rifle : 3-harm near/far 2-ammo loud

Projectiles (choose 1, +1-cred for another) :

  • Acid spray ammo : +1-ap
  • Ammunition nanofab : +retrieve
  • Diamond-plated ammo : +1-ap
  • EMP ammo : +emp
  • Explosive ammo : +1-harm +breach +dangerous
  • Flechette : +flechette (optional : -harm)
  • Grappling : +grappling
  • Hacking vector ammo : +vector
  • High velocity ammo : +1-harm
  • Magnetic ammo : +retrieve
  • Nanobarbed ammo : +2-ap dangerous
  • Particle beam : +1-ap
  • Radioactive ammo : +contamination(radioactivity) +dangerous
  • Seeker ammo : +seeker
  • Shrapnel ammo : +1-harm
  • Spreading ammo : +area

Mods (choose 1, +1-cred for another) :

  • Automatic fire : +automatic
  • Easily repaired : +repair
  • Integrated : it's a 1-aug
  • Long range : +1 range category
  • Magazine surplus : +1-ammo
  • Off grid : +off-grid (+0-cred as an additional mod)
  • Stealthy : -loud +stealthy

Examples :

  • Rapture EV1 : 4-harm close/near automatic 2-ammo 2-cred loud. An automatic gun with high velocity shrapnel ammo commonly used by street thugs.
  • Queen Cobra II : 3-harm +1-ap close/near automatic 1-ammo stealthy retrieve 5-cred. A silent gun loaded with diamond-plated and shrapnel ammo for maximal lethality, several autofires options and magnetic autoretrieve to leave no traces behind. This gun is made of reinforced silk and has a foldable design in order to bypass security checks.
  • Falcon's Eye 4k : 3-harm 2-ap near/far/away seeker 2-ammo stealthy dangerous 3-cred off-grid. A long range sniper with seeker nanobarbed ammo and autocalibrating stabilizers allowing for millimetre precision shots at up to 4km. This sniper can be dismounted in smaller parts to pass security checks.
  • Valhalla 15 : 5-harm breach close/near automatic braced clumsy dangerous 3-ammo loud 1-cred. A huge machine gun with a large magazine for sustained fire and explosive ammo to shoot through walls.

Melee weapons

  • Electrostaff 2-harm emp hand 1-cred : A staff with electromagnetic impulsers at the tips.
  • Monofilament whip 3-harm close mutilate dangerous 1-cred : A handle that releases a monomolecular filament. Very dangerous to use.
  • Vibroknife 2-harm hand 0-cred : A knife whose blade vibrates at ultrasonic frequencies to cut flesh like butter.
  • Vibrosword 3-harm hand 1-cred : A sword with the same technology as the vibroknife.

All these melee weapon can be stealthy 1-aug for +1-cred :

Examples :

  • Mantis blades 3-harm hand stealthy 1-aug : Two vibroblades hidden in each forearm. A common aug for old operatives.
  • Integrated monofilament whip 3-harm close mutilate dangerous stealthy 1-aug : A monofilament whip aug that is generally hidden in a finger.

Grenades

  • Explosive grenade : 4-harm breach area 1-cred
  • Emp grenade : 2-harm emp area 1-cred
  • Sensory overload grenade : overload area 1-cred
  • Nanowire shrapnels grenade : 4-harm 2-ap mutilate breach area dangerous 2-cred

Wearables

Transhuman clothes are highly multifunctional. Standard autocleaning clothes are 0-cred but you may add the following mods :

  • Armored +n-cred : Gives you +n-armor (max +3). If your already have Defence measures, you cannot exceed a total of +3-armor.
  • Exoskeleton +n-cred : Gives you +n-harm (max +3) when inflicting harm with your strength. If you already have Strength enhancements, you cannot exceed a total of +3-harm.
  • Reconfigurable +1-cred : This wearable can change its shape and color to resemble any other wearable.
  • Thermo-optic cammo* +1-cred : The fabric is covered by metamaterials bending electromagnetic radiation in order to make the wearer appear invisible. It gives +1 to Infiltrate*. If you already have Stealth enhancements, it has no effect.
  • Vacsuit +1-cred : Allows their bioshell host to breath oxygen and protect them against the void, oceanic pressures, temperatures in the +-200°C range and standard cosmic radiations. The vacsuit gives you 48 hours of autonomy and recharges automatically when you enter an oxygenated area. You can spend an additional 1-cred to add an additional extreme environment protections : radioactivity, extreme hotness/coldness, ...

Services

Services allow you to roll expertise moves you don't have.

In general, a 1-cred services allow you to roll once (medical assistance, repair, ...) or for the time of a session or the operation (hacking support, a gang, ...) with a +2 bonus.

Here are a list of additional costs :

  • A +3 bonus : +1-cred
  • A +4 bonus : +2-cred
  • Week long services : +1-cred
  • Month long services : +2-cred

For gangs : The base gang is a gang small 2-harm hand/close/near 0-armor. You may choose as much additional tags as the cred you spent to engage the gang. A larger gang is +1-cred by size category.

Gangs

Gang tags

  • small, medium, large : A gang inflicts +1-harm and has +1-armor for each additional size category it has against its opponent.
  • tiny : Does +1-harm but does not have +1-armor when fighting against an individual.
