Moves

Act under pressure [+Variable]

When you act under pressure, choose with the MC if you do it with :

  • Speed and precision : roll +Kinesics.
  • Awareness and perceptiveness : roll +Sensors.
  • Calculations and anticipation : roll +Process.
  • Sociability and manipulation : roll +Memetics.

Success : You do it.

Partial : You do it but you hesitate or flinch. The MC gives you a hard bargain or an undesirable consequence.

Miss : Prepare for the worst.

Facilitate/Complicate [+Variable]

When you act to make things easier or harder for yourself or someone else, choose with the MC if you do it with :

  • Speed, strength and precision : roll +Kinesics.
  • Awareness and perceptiveness : roll +Sensors.
  • Calculations and anticipation : roll +Process.
  • Sociability and manipulation : roll +Memetics.

Success : You give or take +2-forward or -2-forward (you can distribute them).

Partial : You give or take +1-forward or -1-forward (you can distribute them).

Miss : Prepare for the worst.

Virtual reality

When you make a move in virtual reality and the modifier is +Sensors or +Kinesics, you must use +Process instead. Of course, specific virtual realities environments may forbid you altogether to do the move at the MC's discretion.

Infiltrate [+Sensors]

When you try to be stealthy to avoid a problem or pass through an area, roll +Sensors.

Success : You go unnoticed.

Partial : You go unnoticed but the MC chooses 1 :

  • You leave something behind.
  • You take something with you.
  • You have to overcome an obstacle.
  • You have to neutralize something or someone.

Miss : You're detected but you have a small window to prevent the alert to be transmitted.

Deploy violence [+Kinesics]

When you deploy violence, say what you want and roll +Kinesics.

Success : Choose 2 :

  • You inflict +1-harm.
  • You suffer -1-harm.
  • Take control of something/someone/someplace.
  • Force someone to move where you want or stop moving.
  • Take or give +1-forward.

Partial : Choose 1 above and the MC chooses above against you or below :

  • You spend 1-ammo.
  • You attract attention.

Miss : Prepare for the worst.

Exchange harm as established.

Suffer harm [+Harm]

When you suffer harm, roll +Harm suffered (after armor is substracted) against a difficulty of 1.

"Success" : Choose 1 :

  • You're out of action : unconscious, trapped, lost, glitched or panicked.
  • The harm is ap. If you already suffered all harm, you suffer +1-harm.
  • You loose a limb (MC chooses). You loose all relevant augs and locomotion modes.

"Partial" : MC chooses 1 :

  • One of your locomotion modes stops working.
  • One of your aug breaks : +broken.
  • Take -1-ongoing until you can rest.

"Miss" : You endure the damage.

Fast talk [+Memetics]

When you argue, fast talk or persuade someone of something credible, roll +Memetics.

Success : They go along with until something contradicts you.

Partial : They'll go along until something contradicts you and if you first give them a credible corroboration.

Miss : They don't believe you and see that you lie if you are.

Negotiate [+Memetics]

When you negotiate something with someone in exchange of something they want and it's unsure if they want to do it for you.

Success : They do it if you promise them something they want.

Partial : They do it if you give them a guarantee first.

Miss : If they're neutral or friendly, they do it if you first give them what they want. If they have something against you, prepare for the worst.

Analyze behavior [+Memetics]

While you're interacting with someone and analyse their behaviour, roll +Memetics.

Success : Ask 3.

Partial : Ask 1.

Miss : Ask 1 but your target analysed you as well (MC may ask questions).

Questions :

  • Are they honest ?
  • What does they want ?
  • What are they feeling right now ?
  • Which memes do they adhere to ?
  • How could I get them to ___ ?

Take +1-forward if you act based on the answers.

Assess a situation [+Sensors]

When you assess a situation, roll +Sensors.

Success : Ask 3.

Partial : Ask 1.

Miss : Ask 1 anyway but prepare for the worst.

Questions :

  • What are the technologies here ?
  • What's a potential complication ?
  • What's hidden here ?
  • How ___ is vulnerable to me ?
  • How can I avoid trouble ?
  • What's the best way in/out/past ?
  • What's the biggest threat here ?
  • What augs do ___ have ?

Take +1-forward if you act based on the answers.

Investigate [+Process]

When you take time to research data on the Grid or in real life, roll +Process.

Success : Ask 3.

Partial : Ask 1.

Miss : Ask 1 but you attracted attention.

Questions :

  • What does ___ means ?
  • What is ___ known for ?
  • How are and related ?
  • Who owned or employed ___ ?
  • What place is related to ___ ?
  • To whom ___ is valuable ?
  • How is ___ secured ?

Take +1-forward if you act based on the answers.

Get in touch [+Memetics]

Once per session, you can declare a contact. Say who they are, what they do and where they are.

Success : The owe you one (you detail).

Partial : You know them for sure but it'll be the normal price.

Miss : You owe them one (MC details).

Suffer trauma [+Trauma]

When you suffer trauma, roll your total trauma (the trauma you had + the trauma you just suffered) against a difficulty of 1.

"Success" : You loose control of yourself (MC says what you do). You may or may not have memories of what you did.

"Partial" : Choose 1 :

  • You have -1-ongoing until you can chill out.
  • You loose track of something.

Reshell [+Trauma]

When you wake up in a new shell, roll your current trauma against an difficulty of 1 if your new shell is similar, a difficulty of 2 if different and a difficulty of 3 if the new shell is exotic or horrific.

Success : You accept your reshelling.

Partial : You have a hard time accepting this shell. You have -1-ongoing until you've done something to feel ok in this shell.

Miss : You freak out. You suffer 1-trauma and have -1-ongoing until you've done something to feel ok in this shell.

Resist hacking [+Process]

When you're getting hacked, roll +Process.

Success : Your antivirus manages to contain the penetration.

Partial : Choose between going off-grid or not. If you stay connected, the MC chooses 2. If you go off-grid, the MC chooses 1 :

  • One of your augs, items or locomotion mode breaks (MC chooses) : +broken.
  • You suffer 1-harm ap.
  • Your wallet is penetrated : you loose 1-cred.
  • You suffer 1-trauma.
  • The virus is resilient, it stays hidden and may reactivate at the MC's discretion (in this case, you must roll Resist hacking again).
  • A spyware is deployed and captures your data.
  • The damage is irreparable : +destroyed instead of +broken.
  • You overload their sensory inputs : +overload.
  • They get a fork of your ego.

Miss : You're infected, the MC chooses 3 above or 1 below :

  • You're out of action : unconscious, glitched or deactivated.
  • The assailant controls your shell : when they force your shell to do something, you may Act under pressure with +Process to take back control or mitigate results.

Design [+Process]

This move is not available by default but is available through specific Design moves.

When you want to build, repair or modify something, tell what you want to do. The MC will say you if you can and may choose as much as they want :

  • It will take minutes/hours/days/weeks/months.
  • You will need a space to work.
  • You will need special materials (which ?).
  • You will need special tools (which ?).
  • You will need special data (which ?).
  • You will need extensive danger management measures (which ?).
  • You will need to get ___ .

When you have everything needed, roll +Process.

Success : You design it and it works.

Partial : You have a working prototype but the MC chooses 1 :

  • It has a minor defect.
  • It may malfunction or break at some point.
  • It may have costed more resources.
  • It only has the basic functionalities.

Miss* : You have a barely working prototype, it will break or malfunction very soon. When you use it, Act under pressure*** with +Process to see what happens.
