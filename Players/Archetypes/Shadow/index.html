The Shadow

You're a freelancing operative with a specific set of skills.

Expertises

At character creation, choose 2 expertises : - Droning - Hacking - Psychosurgery

Get the expertises' gear and assets, gigs and special moves.

Background

What about you ? - You are an activist : choose the Activist background. - You were in a criminal organization : choose the Criminal background. - You were built for it : choose the Construct background. - You come from somewhere where things are different : choose the Outer background. - You were a soldier in another life : choose the Veteran background.

Angel

Your angel is : - A pseudofork of yourself. - A pseudofork of someone you were close to (who are they and what happened to them ?). - A AI with a personality based on someone famous (who ?). - An AI with a personality tuned to your taste (how did you program them to be ?)

Shell

As your starting shell, you have (choose 1) : - An infoshell with +2 Process and +2 Memetics : say where your MPU is. - A bioshell with 3-aug (choose which). - A synthshell with 3-aug (choose which).

If you're not in your original body, you start with 1-trauma. If you have reshelled a lot before, you start with 2-trauma.

Gear and assets

At character creation you have : - A weapon worth 3-cred. - Your angel is a daemon. It automatically fakes your identifications and covers your track when in a public space. Contrary to most people, your life is not public data.

Gigs

Your gigs depends on your expertises but as a freelancing operative, it generally involves illegal missions such as infiltration, exfiltration, investigation, theft, kidnapping, assassination, ...
