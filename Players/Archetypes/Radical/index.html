The Radical

Name

A name you took to defend your cause, a name you were given to defend your cause.

The Cause

Choose your Cause :

  • A new religion : You're a zealot for a new religion, which one ?
  • An old religion from earth : You're a zealot for an old religion, which one ?
  • Anarcho-capitalism / Minarchism : You think welfare and authoritarian states shoud be abolished.
  • Anti-hypercapitalism : You think hypercapitalism should be destroyed.
  • Anti-specism : You think all biological species should have rights.
  • Artificial sentience rights : You think artificial sentiences and lifeforms should be accepted as citizens.
  • ___ .

What does your Cause entails ? What makes of you an extremist even for this Cause ? How does your Cause judges society ? How do you differentiate between allies and enemies to the Cause ?

The Community

What are your community's strengths ? (choose 2) :

  • They are vocal on the Grid.
  • They have sub-communities in most habitats.
  • They have a lot of money and resources.
  • They are secrets and decentralized.
  • They are politically soft but mostly accepted by society.
  • They have habitats socially designed around your Cause.

What are your community's weaknesses ? (choose ...) :

  • They are always arguing with each other.
  • They are poors and have few resources.
  • They are isolated.
  • They are few.

What's the commonest interpretation of your Cause amongst the community ? How do you think they are wrong about it and should be more radical in their ideas ? What are the subgroups and tendencies inside the community ? Where do they meet ? What are their symbols ?

Radical moves

By default, you get the Social change move.

Social change

When you come in a habitat, create a new social change clock for this habitat, ask the MC how this habitat positions around your Cause.

  • If they're neutral, the Social change clock starts with 3 track filled.
  • If they're mostly hostile, the Social change clock starts with 1 or 2 tracks filled.
  • If they're deadly hostile, the Social change clock starts with 0 tracks filled.

During play, situations will add or remove tracks to the Social change clock :

  • When you show to the public the damage done by enemies to the Cause, add 1 track.
  • When you give the people what they want, add 1 track.
  • When you give the powers what they want, add 1 track.
  • When you severely hurts the interests of enemies to the Cause, add 1 track.
  • When a respected figure takes your Cause side, add 1 track.
  • When yourself or your community publicly makes fools of themselves, remove 1 track.
  • When your or your community's interests severely suffers, remove 1 track.
  • When a respected figure takes side against your Cause, remove 1 track.

If the Social change clock hits 6 tracks, you bring social change in this habitat : laws are passed, historical events happen, power is transferred, ... Social change is not always a clean process, your Cause wins, but there may be costs to be paid.

If the Social change clock hits 0 tracks, your community is hunted by authorities as priority criminals.

If you move to another habitat, create a new Social change clock for this habitat.

Community [+Memetics]

When you ask something from your community, roll +Memetics.

Success : They give it to you if they can.

Partial : They give it to you if they can but the MC chooses 1 : - It's not exactly what you asked. - It attracted an undesirable attention. - It created drama in your community : you have -1 forward the next time you use this move.

Miss : They don't have it and prepare for the worst.

Crowdfunded [+Memetics]

Militants from your Community fund you to bring social change. You gain 2-cred when you add 1 track to a Social change clock. You gain +2-cred if it hits 6 tracks.

Famous [+Memetics]

When someone already knows you (you say or the MC says), roll +Memetics.

Success : You say what they've heard of you.

Partial : You say something they've heard of you and the MC adds something.

Miss : The MC says what they've heard of you.

Preach [+Memetics]

When you preach the Cause to a neutral or friendly crowd, roll +Memetics.

Success : Hold 3.

Partial : Hold 1.

Miss : Prepare for the worst.

Spend your hold 1 for 1 : - A NPC becomes friendly or loyal to your Cause. - The crowd does something collectively (protest, celebrate, share, testify, give up to an emotion). - You can ask a question from Read a person about the crowd or someone in it. - You don't make enemies. - The crowd goes back to their business.

Squad

You have joined or created a small cell of operatives with various expertises dedicated to your Cause. They can throw any expertise move with a +2 bonus for you or serve as a gang small.

Sworn enemy

When you directly act against an enemy of the Cause or to further your Cause, you have +1 ongoing.
