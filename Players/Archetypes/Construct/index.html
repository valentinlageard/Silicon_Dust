Construct

Characteristics

By default, you have +1 in all your core characteristic. Your shell characteristics depends on your augs.

Humanity

The Construct in in search of its purpose and transhumanity is its only model. As such, the Construct gains and spend Humanity according to its moves. You start with 0 Humanity.

Humanity : ___

Angel

You do not have an angel.

Shell

At character creation you have (choose 1) :

  • An infoshell with +2 Process.
  • A synthshell indiscernible from a human from the outside with 3-aug.
  • A artificial looking synthshell with 3-aug.
  • A human bioshell with 3-aug.

Spend your aug to select your starting augmentations (see Gear).

Gear

You start with no gear and cred.

Expertise

At character creation, choose an expertise and get its Gear and assets, Gigs and Moves.

Connections

Choose one of the other players. When the game starts, you awaken somewhere with no memory, they are the first people you meet and the first human you study (see the Humans move).

XP and Improvements

When you roll a highlighted characteristic or if a move tells you to, you win XP. When you have 5 XP, you may choose one of the following improvement :

  • Get a new move from your archetype.
  • Get a new move from your expertise.

After your fifth improvement, you may also choose :

  • Increase by 1 your core characteristic (max +2).
  • Get a new expertise.

Construct moves

At character creation, you have the Remember, Humans and Stigma moves. You may choose other Construct moves as improvements.

Remember

Whenever you spend 1 Humanity to answer a question about yourself, roll +3 against 1. The MC may ask you or not to do something before to unlock the question.

Success : The MC chooses the question and you answer it.

Partial : You choose the question and the MC answers it.

Miss : The MC chooses the question and answers it.

Questions :

  • [ ] What's my true first memory ?
  • [ ] When was I created ?
  • [ ] What do I remember of my creator(s) ?
  • [ ] How do I perceive my own mind ?
  • [ ] Are there others like me ?
  • [ ] Was I created in the image of someone ?
  • [ ] Did I commit crimes before ? What were they ?
  • [ ] What was my original purpose ?
  • [ ] Does humanity fascinates me ? Why ?

When you answered all the questions, you have a Revelation :

  • Realize humanity is only a step and you're the next one. Leave humanity to become something different. This character becomes a NPC and you create a new character.
  • Realize humanity is a disease and you're the cure. This character becomes a NPC and you create a new character.
  • Realize you are or may become a human too. You loose all your Construct moves and choose a new archetype.
  • Realize your existence has no meaning and kill yourself.
  • Realize you're not human and you have a grand mission (say what it is). You may stay a Construct for now or loose all Construct moves and take a new relevant archetype and may choose another Revelation later.

Humans

When you decide to study a particular transhuman to understand what makes them human, follow them around. When you think you have figured it out, tell them of something that makes them human or inhuman. If they agree, you gain 2 Humanity, if they disagree, you gain 1 Humanity.

Stigma

You don't know it yet but most of the societies are against artificial lifeforms. In these societies, being artificial may imply to be property or may simply imply forced deactivation. You will learn it the hard way.

Judgement

When you witness humanity at its best or at its worst, tell them and gain 1 Humanity.

Learn

You can spend 1 Humanity to roll any of the expertise moves.

Better than human

You can spend 1 Humanity to treat a Miss as a Partial or a Partial as a Success.

Worse than human

When you reveal your artificiality to Intimidate or Deploy violence, you can spend 1 Humanity to have an automatic success.

Different

You're invulnerable to Trauma. Erase all your current Trauma tracks and you never have to Suffer trauma or suffer the counter-effects when you Reshell.
